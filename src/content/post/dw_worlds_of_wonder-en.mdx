This turned into a much better day than expected.

The plan was just to spend an afternoon at the San Diego Museum of Art. The sky was gray, the paths were still wet, and Balboa Park felt unusually quiet. Then, while walking past the Comic-Con Museum, I looked up and saw a row of very familiar blue boxes with `Doctor Who Worlds of Wonder` across them.

I had no idea this exhibition was there. It was not the reason for the trip, and I definitely had not planned around it. I just happened to walk by at the right moment.

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2393.jpeg" alt="Comic-Con Museum at Balboa Park" style="width: 100%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
<p style="text-align: center; font-style: italic; color: gray;">The Doctor Who banner outside Comic-Con Museum</p>

I walked over just to make sure it was real.

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2394.jpeg" alt="Doctor Who Worlds of Wonder flyer" style="width: 70%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
<p style="text-align: center; font-style: italic; color: gray;">Exhibition flyer</p>

Tickets were about $30 for adults and $20 for students. At the time, signing up on the official site also got a $15 discounted ticket.

## That Blue Door

The entrance itself was pretty plain, but the second that blue door came into view, the whole place snapped into Doctor Who mode.

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2398.jpeg" alt="Exhibition entrance shaped like the TARDIS" style="width: 100%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
<p style="text-align: center; font-style: italic; color: gray;">Exhibition entrance</p>

Right inside was a wall of costumes.

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2400.jpeg" alt="Doctor costumes from modern eras" style="width: 100%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
<p style="text-align: center; font-style: italic; color: gray;">Modern Doctor costumes</p>

Nine, Ten, Eleven, Twelve, Thirteen. If you have been with the show for years, seeing them lined up still does something to you. Twelve's outfit hit especially hard. It pulled me straight back to 2014, when I first started watching.

## Props

Further in were the console and the sonic screwdrivers[^1]. The console was close enough to actually put a hand on, which I obviously did.

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2406.jpeg" alt="TARDIS console" style="width: 100%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
<p style="text-align: center; font-style: italic; color: gray;">TARDIS console</p>

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2408.jpeg" alt="A display of sonic screwdrivers" style="width: 80%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
<p style="text-align: center; font-style: italic; color: gray;">sonic screwdrivers</p>

One display case held smaller props that do not show up very often, including Twelve's sonic shades[^2] and the little device used to identify the Zygon Queen Elizabeth in the 50th anniversary special[^3].

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2410.jpeg" alt="Props and gadgets from Doctor Who" style="width: 80%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />

There was also Handles[^4][^5], the old Cyberman head that stayed with Eleven through the end. Nice to see him there.

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2411.jpeg" alt="Handles from The Time of the Doctor" style="width: 55%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />

Past the outfit section was a row of three spacesuits. The one on the left is River Song's Apollo-style suit from the `S06` arc[^6]. The middle one is from `S04E08-S04E09`[^7][^8], and seeing it immediately brought back `Hey, who turned out the lights?`. The orange suit on the right is the classic one that keeps showing up all over the series, including Clara's in `S08E07 Kill the Moon`[^9].

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2416.jpeg" alt="Space suits in the exhibition" style="width: 100%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />

When I walked out, it still was not dark over Balboa Park. The ground was wet, but the light was starting to come back.
