这天真是太有趣了.

本来只是想去 San Diego Museum of Art 逛逛. 天有点阴, 地面还是湿的, 整个 Balboa Park 都安安静静的. 路过 Comic-Con Museum 的时候一抬头, 忽然看见再熟悉不过的蓝盒子, 上面写着 `Doctor Who Worlds of Wonder`.

之前完全不知道这里有这个展. 不是专门来看, 也没提前做功课, 就是路过时刚好撞上了.

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2393.jpeg" alt="Comic-Con Museum at Balboa Park" style="width: 100%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
<p style="text-align: center; font-style: italic; color: gray;">Comic-Con Museum 外墙上的 Doctor Who 横幅</p>

走近一看, 还真是.

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2394.jpeg" alt="Doctor Who Worlds of Wonder flyer" style="width: 70%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
<p style="text-align: center; font-style: italic; color: gray;">展览传单</p>

票价大概是成人 30 美元, 学生 20 美元. 当时如果在官网注册过活动信息, 还有 15 美元优惠票.

## That Blue Door

展览入口本身很简单, 但蓝门一摆在那里, 味道一下子就对了.

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2398.jpeg" alt="Exhibition entrance shaped like the TARDIS" style="width: 100%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
<p style="text-align: center; font-style: italic; color: gray;">展览入口</p>

进去先看到一整面服装墙.

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2400.jpeg" alt="Doctor costumes from modern eras" style="width: 100%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
<p style="text-align: center; font-style: italic; color: gray;">新版 Doctor 的服装</p>

九任到十三任都在. 追了这么多年, 再看到这些衣服还是会立刻停下来. 尤其是十二任那套, 一下子把记忆拽回 2014 年刚开始追剧的时候.

## 道具区

再往里就是控制台和 sonic screwdrivers[^1]. 控制台甚至还能上手摸一下.

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2406.jpeg" alt="TARDIS console" style="width: 100%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
<p style="text-align: center; font-style: italic; color: gray;">TARDIS 控制台</p>

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2408.jpeg" alt="A display of sonic screwdrivers" style="width: 80%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
<p style="text-align: center; font-style: italic; color: gray;">sonic screwdrivers</p>

旁边展柜里放着一些没那么常见的道具, 有十二叔的音速眼镜[^2], 还有五十周年纪念集中用来探查 Zygon Queen Elizabeth 的小道具[^3].

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2410.jpeg" alt="Props and gadgets from Doctor Who" style="width: 80%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
<p style="text-align: center; font-style: italic; color: gray;">零碎道具</p>

还有 `The Time of the Doctor` 里的 Handles[^4][^5]. 陪 11 任走完最后一段的老朋友, 单独摆在那里还挺让人感慨.

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2411.jpeg" alt="Handles from The Time of the Doctor" style="width: 55%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
<p style="text-align: center; font-style: italic; color: gray;">Handles</p>

再往前是一排三套宇航服. 左边这套是 River Song[^6] 在 `S06` 故事线里的 Apollo 风宇航服. 中间这套来自图书馆两集 `S04E08-S04E09`[^7][^8], 看到就会自动想起那句 "Hey, who turned out the lights?". 右边这套橙色宇航服则是经常出现的经典款, 比如 Clara 在 `S08E07 Kill the Moon` 穿过的那一套[^9].

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2416.jpeg" alt="Space suits in the exhibition" style="width: 100%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
<p style="text-align: center; font-style: italic; color: gray;">三套宇航服</p>

左边的 Weeping Angel 来自 `S03E10`[^10], 右边这只毛茸茸的 Meep 则来自 2023 年 60 周年 `Special 1`[^11].

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2419.jpeg" alt="Weeping Angel and the Meep" style="width: 80%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2508.jpeg" alt="Comic-Con Museum exterior after the visit" style="width: 100%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
<p style="text-align: center; font-style: italic; color: gray;">离开展馆时再回头看一眼</p>

<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" src="https://cdn.lyt0112.com/Posts/dw_worlds_of_wonder/IMG_2511.jpeg" alt="Balboa Park amphitheater" style="width: 100%; height: auto; display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
